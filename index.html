Visit Ibadan

Enjoy rich culture and mouth-watering delicacies in this ancient city, which is the largest in West Africa.

Top three activities to do at Ibadan

Visit any of the Amala joints in Ibadan

Ibadan is one of the best places you can truly enjoy the savoury of Amala, especially with Ewedu and Gbegiri

Climb the Bower's Memorial Tower

From this tower, referred to as 'Oke Layipo' because of its spiral stairs, you can view the entire city of Ibadan.

Visit the IITA Forest Reserve

This Reserve preserves over 860 bird, butterfly and plants species used for a range of purposes.
